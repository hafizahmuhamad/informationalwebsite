POPULAR FESTIVALS


Every year, Kelantan will organize a festival as a way to attract tourists to come here. Heritage, culture and food are the main attractions tourist to visit Kota Bharu.

Kota Bharu is well known as the cradle of the Malay Culture. 70 % of the population in the Kota Bharu came from the ethnic Malay. The tradition of the Malay cultures still nourished in Kota Bharu. During festivals, there will be a lot of entertainment and performance from local artist. It is fun and entertaining ways for people to get together and celebrate these things.


1) KELANTAN INTERNATIONAL WAU FESTIVAL


Since wau is synonym with the stste of Kelantan, the Kelantan's goverment were organize an International Wau Festival every year. Usually the show will be held at Genting Beach, Tumpat, Kelantan for 3 days. This festival to ensures that the tradition of kite-flying is inherited by the younger generations and experienced by tourists. The festival brings together all the international participant from variety countries including Scotland, Sweden, Colombia, Korea, Germany Brunei, China and Thailand to show their creativity on wau. The best part about this festival is you will be amazed when you see the variety types of wau or kite flying in the air.


2) FOOD THAI FESTIVAL


Food Thai Festival usually will be held at Sultan Muhamamad IV Stadium for several days. Over 100 menus of foods, beverages and drinks are available and it were selling by original seller from neighbour countries which is Thailand. This is an opportunity for people around country to tastes the diversity food provided with affordable price. In addition, the festival also an initiative to attract more foreign tourists to visit Kelantan to tastes the variety cuisine. Not only interesting menus but the visitors were also delighted with cultural events, performance and games throughout the festival.
