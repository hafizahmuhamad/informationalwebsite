POPULAR CULTURES AND TRADITIONAL GAMES



As we know, Kelantan is very familiar with the uniqueness of culture and variety of traditional games which is can be an attractions to invite more people to came here. Each cultures and games have its own style and methods. Usually it will be played during ceremony.

If the visitors want to see more heritage culture, they can visit cultural centre on Jalan Mahmud Kota Bharu where they can see variable showcase of the living heritage of Kelantan.

So here there is a few about cultures and games in Kelantan.



1) DIKIR BARAT

Dikir Barat is one of Kelantan's trademark. It is usually played by ten to twenty people. It can be perform either by solo or as a group. They will sing, clap their hands and move their body following the rhythm of the traditional music. The main Character in this performance is Tok Juara and Tok Karut.
2) WAYANG KULIT

Wayang kulit is a traditional game played by the kelantanese and usually it is played in the ward (bangsal) or a small cinema that is complement with traditional song music and Wayang Kulit dolls. Different dolls have different roles. Tok Dalang is the one who move and play all the role but he have to use different tone of voice.The story that is played is usually based on the classic stories from Kelantan History. It is originally from hindu but then it is change to follow the local tradition and culture.
3) WAU

Wau Bulan is the most popular kite in Kelantan. It called Wau Bulan because it take after the shape of the moon and tail also resembles a cresent. It is usually played after the harvesting season. It is called 'Wau' because the shape of its wing is similar to an Arabic letter (pronounced "wow"). The strong wind would indicate the time for Wau festivals. Wau festivals usually will be held on beach and the most thrill part is visitors can watching variety design and type of wau that indicating creativity and ingenuity. Playing kite is about satisfied watching the kite soar in the skies. Wau is sell along the road to Cahaya Bulan Beach.

Types of Wau:

  • Wau Bulan
  • Wau Merak
  • Wau Budi

4) GASING

Gasing or tops spinning, is a traditonal games popular among Malay (Native people). Gasing has come out with unique design and it really have their own class. Especially amongst the villagers in the State of Kelantan and Melaka. Each season after the rice harvest,several villagers will come together to challenge each other in an ultimate test of skill. Some masters are so skillful where they can make the tops spin for hours. The villagers believed that the spinning tops would help bring good harvest. Since this outdoor game is difficult to find, the visitor can visit Gasing industries is in Kampung Ana Jal Besar in Tumpat, Kota Bharu and Bachok.

Types of gasing:

  • Gasing Uri - made from metal (logam timah) and Merbau wooden.
  • Gasing Pangkah - made by Keranji wooden

5) CONGKAK

Congkak is a popular Malay traditional game that is often played as indoor activities during leisure time. Congkak consist of congkak board, which is usually a long piece of wood with two rows of and have seven holes on each side and in the hole there will have marbles or seeds. This game requires two players share a same wooden board. The game ends when a player have collecting more marbles in their 'house'.


6) SENI SILAT TARI

Seni Silat is one of the famous Malay heritage in Kelantan. Silat is a Malay martial arts that can be seen through the moves and usually there will be accompaniment music . In addition, there are variety types of Silat such as Silat Gayong, Silat Cekak and Silat Pulut, and it have different types of movement. Usually it will be perform at formal and festive occasions like weddings. Silat performance is held at the ' Gelanggang Seni, Kelantan once a week on certain days like Monday and Wednesday.
